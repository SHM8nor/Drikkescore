Usage Guidelines

  • Prussian Blue: Primary brand color. Use for main navigation, primary buttons, and important text.
  • Fire Engine Red: Reserved for errors and destructive actions. Don't overuse.
  • Orange Wheel: Use for CTAs and highlights. Creates visual energy and draws attention.
  • Xanthous: Secondary accent. Good for warnings and info badges. Avoid for body text due to contrast.
  • Vanilla: Subtle background color. Use for page backgrounds and card backgrounds.
